A website in a lightweight desktop environment

This website was made in Sublime, Sublime is a lightweight integrated developing environment, which is why I chose it for SMD (Sam's Minimal Desktop) combination, this integrates nicely into lxpanel and openbox which is good for me and my laptop, ever since I used this SMD, my laptop has been running faster because it doesn't waste resources on XFCE.

Conclusion: its not hard to switch to a minimal desktop and people should do it more often

Problems and adapting to a minimal desktop

However I have noticed a slight problem after using Sublime text, It selects text when I accidentally touch my mousepad (or whatever it is called) and it might delete a lot of important text that I have just spent 5 minutes writing, I also need to switch to a Dvorak Layout or change my typing habits to limit Repititive Strain Injuries.

Conclusion: its hard to adapt to a minimal desktop environment but it takes time and people should also try to limit repititive strain injuries by using a dvorak layout or changing typing/keyboard habits or just by not using the computer a lot.

But most important of all, Take care of yourself, a happy body is a happy soul

Especially because when it comes to Linux, because everything can go downhill really quick
